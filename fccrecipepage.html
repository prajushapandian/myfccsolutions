Pizza

Welcome to the ultimate guide for making the perfect pizza for your late night cravings ;)

Ingredients

  • Cheese
  • Flour
  • Water
  • Yeast
  • Tomato
  • Basil
  • Garlic

Instructions

  1. Prepare your dought by mixing flour, water and yeast
  2. Let the dough rest for half an hour
  3. Prepare the sauce by adding garlic,tomato and basil to a pot
  4. Roll out the dough
  5. Spread the sauce on top of the dough
